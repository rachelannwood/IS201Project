    5 Fascinating Facts About Capybaras

  1. Capybaras are calm & friendly by nature
  2. Guinea pigs are their closest relative
  3. They are native to the following locations:
    • Brasil
    • Columbia
    • Bolivia
    • Paraguay
  4. Like other rodents, their teeth never stop growing!
  5. They whistle, bark, & purr to communicate
