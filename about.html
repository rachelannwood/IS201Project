About Me

My name is Rachel Wood and I’m a pre-business major hoping to join the EXDM program!

I've always had a passion for planning and celebrating events. The experiences I had growing up in Indiana as the “party planner” of my friend group and while on my mission in São Paulo, Brazil taught me that this is a lifelong interest of mine. It wasn’t till I took my first EXDM 300 class and was planning my wedding however that I realized I could turn that passion into a career!

I love event planning and bringing people together, as well as creating positive experiences, which drives my passion for the field of EXDM. I'm finding that my love for creating meaningful experiences grows with each event I attend and I look forward to every opportunity that furthers my ability to enhance the marketing of such special experiences as products.

When I’m not in class or busy coordinating weddings & events at my current job, I love to watch mystery shows with my husband (SILO being our current favorite), gardening, traveling, and making, but mostly eating, traditional Brazilian food.
